## What's solid for go-live

The blocker is **restart resilience (#673)**; once a restarted node resumes its answerer, plus the #671 dashboard-parity fix, this is go-live ready (TURN-on-k8s remains a separate hardening item for arbitrary symmetric-NAT parties).
